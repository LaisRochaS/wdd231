Essential Winter Hiking Gear

Proper gear can make your winter hiking experience safer, warmer, and more enjoyable. Here's what you should pack and wear on every cold-weather hike.

Insulated Clothing

Layer up! Wear a thermal base layer, fleece or down insulation, and a waterproof, windproof outer shell.

Winter Hiking Boots

Choose insulated, waterproof boots with good traction. Gaiters help keep snow out of your boots.

Trekking Poles

Poles with snow baskets improve balance and reduce strain on icy or uneven terrain.

Traction Devices

Microspikes, crampons, or snowshoes are essential for slippery or deep snow conditions.

Navigation Tools

Emergency Kit
